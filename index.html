Products and Services
Manufactured for the Industrial Marketplace

Temco has been a tier one supplier to the transportation industry since 1966. An ISO 9001 company, vertically integrated to guarantee quality and on-time delivery throughout production. Consistently innovating the art of high-volume production, Temco can also meet the needs of customers with unique applications.

With over 50,000 sq ft, Temco continues to expand into new industries, providing products to the medical, agriculture, and marine markets.

Product Line

With over fifty years experience as an Original Equipment Manufacturer in Heavy Duty Truck fuel tank components, Temco provides both standard and custom designed components that meet and exceed Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration (FMCSA) requirements.

What we do

All Capabilities
DIE CASTING
Die Cast Mold Design and Fabrication
METAL STAMPING
Blanking and forming, Die Design, and Fabrication upon request.
FABRICATION
Temco combines our expertise in stamping, welding, die casting, and tube bending to produce finished fabrications per customer specifications.
PRODUCT TESTING
Temco offers a variety of product testing tailored to your needs, whether in-process or as finished products.
ENGINEERING
Temco offers custom engineering and new product design backed by its fifty years of experience as an OEM supplier to the Transportation Industry and ISO 9001:2015 compliance.
CNC MACHINING
Temco's CNC machining department consists of nine lathes and mills: Turning in diameters up to 5", Screw machine capacity up to 1.25", Vertical milling with working envelopes up to 20" x 24".
PRODUCTION WELDING
Custom Weldment and Production Fabrication. Certified welding with TIG and MIG, Specialized welding of aluminum, Computerized, digital Fronius welding equipment,
IN HOUSE TOOLING & PROTOTYPING
Quick iterative design to reduce time to market. Temco has a staff of skilled tool and die makers producing all in-house tooling and fixturing for prototype concepts and high-yield production runs.
TUBE BENDING
Temco utilizes mandrel bending and high volume bend wing dies to bend tubing in diameters of 1/8"-5" in a variety of metals.
