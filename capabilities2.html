Die Casting

  • Prince die cast machines in 450 and 600 ton capacities.
  • In house die cast mold design and fabrication
  • In house die maintenance and replacement
  • Cold chamber injection using 369, 380, 413, and 518 aluminum alloys
  • Additional alloys per customer need
  • Secondary machining & welding capabilities
  • Secondary precision assembly available

Metal Stamping

  • All stamping dies designed, constructed, and maintained in-house
  • Progressive, blank, form, draw, and single operation dies
  • Mechanical as well as pneumatic assembly operations
  • Turret press capability
  • Coil feeders

Fabrication

  • Shearing up to 12 feet
  • Press brake operations up to 10 feet
  • Slip roll up to 8.5 feet
  • Turret press with 5'x8' bed
  • Roll forming
  • Custom weld fixturing
  • Tube bending
  • Tube beading
  • Tank manufacturing
  • Custom weldment and production fabrication

Engineering

Temco offers custom engineering and new product design backed by its fifty years of experience as an OEM supplier to the Transportation Industry and ISO 9001:2015 compliance.
Full-service Engineering provides ongoing product improvement and cost reduction. Early engineering involvement in customer designs has reduced cost and sped time to market for Temco's customers.

Product Testing

  • Flow testing
  • Leak testing of assemblies, gaskets, and weld joints
  • Liquid fuel tank drop testing per the requirements of FMCSA section 393.67
  • Environmental chamber testing -100° to 10° C
  • Component and assembly endurance testing
  • Fusible material testing
  • Tensile testing up to 100 kN

CNC Machining

Temco's CNC machining department consists of nine lathes and mills:
  • Turning in diameters up to 5"
  • Screw machine capacity up to 1.25"
  • Vertical milling with working envelopes up to 20" x 24"
  • Programming via Mastercam

Production Welding

  • Certified welding with TIG and MIG
  • Specialized welding of aluminum
  • Welding of carbon and stainless steel with GMAW, FCAW, and GTAW
  • Computerized, digital Fronius welding equipment
  • Extensive documented welding procedures & records
  • Continuous welder performance evaluation and training
  • Industry leader in leak-free welding of die cast components
  • Semi-automated welding for high throughput solutions

House Tooling & Prototyping

Temco has a staff of skilled tool and die makers producing all in-house tooling and fixturing for prototype concepts and high-yield production runs. CNC Lathes, EDM, and 3D printing capabilities produce efficient prototype-to-production manufactured goods.
Temco is equipped to provide you with quick turnaround times and cost savings for your prototype needs. Our tooling experts can concurrently produce the dies and fixtures necessary for volume manufacturing.

Tube Bending

Temco utilizes mandrel bending and high volume bend wing dies to bend tubing in diameters of 1/8"-5" in a variety of metals. Materials:
  • Steel
  • Stainless steel
  • Aluminum
  • Brass
  • Other metals per request
Temco can perform secondary operations to your tubing in our stamping, welding, CNC, and assembly cells.
