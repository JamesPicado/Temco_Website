Established In 1966 As An OEM Supplier
To Heavy Duty Truck Manufacturers.

Since 1966, Temco Engineered Products, Inc. has designed and manufactured a full line of tank components for the transportation industry, including the trucking OEMs of North America. Temco has become an international expert in fuel tanks and fuel tank components. Temco is ISO 9001:2015 certified to meet our customer quality requirements. Temco provides on-time delivery, short lead times, and quality products that meet federal 393.67 requirements for our customers.

Temco continues to supply 90% of the fuel tank components to the North American heavy duty truck industry. Our expertise in aluminum, steel, and stainless steel has allowed for rapid expansion into new markets including medical, agriculture, and marine markets to name a few.




We take pride in being a full service product design and manufacturing company. Temco's commitment to quality and vertical integration ensures competitive pricing, consistent innovation, and control of quality from raw material to finished goods.

Our philosophy is to continue to develop our business based on a culture of exceeding our customer’s expectations. We always aspire to be your supplier of choice; given the opportunity, you will see why this has become the case with our existing customers. Temco Engineered Products, Inc. is headquartered in Clackamas, Oregon. All of our components are proudly made in the USA.

Temco offers over 50,000 square feet of manufacturing capabilities including:

  • Die Casting
  • CNC Machining
  • Stamping
  • Fabrication
  • Precision MIG Welding
  • Precision TIG Welding
  • Tooling
  • Rapid Prototyping
  • Product Testing
  • Precision Assembly
